WeGame is a web app that help gamers and game developers in many aspects:
  • Gamers can get game recommendation predicted by AI according to their gaming history and get sales for indie games that are sponsored by WeGame.co. Users get bonus points for playing games sponsored by us. Each bonus points equals some amount of money so gamers can earn money just by playing games. Prizes will be supported by our foundation.
  • Developers can advertise their games on our platform and test their betas with users. The games that are sponsored by us will earn many users and more money. And they'll pay a little part of the money that they earned from our website back to us after reaching per milestone. Foundation will be supported by joining fee and the money that comes from sponsors.

The main mission is to make a web app that all the gamers or game developers can use according to their puposes. Game developers will be able to connect to the gamers and get feedback and reviews. We aim to help indie game developers through brand new digital platform. With this platform, gamers will be able to get game recommendations and gaming tips by AI used system. Other than that we have community of gamers that will help gamer users to enjoy their time. Also, we are planning to support indie game development studios that are advertised by us will have a chance to increase user count or get many users via advertisement. After they reach per milestone (according to money that they earned and users count) they will pay us some money. most part of this money will be spent on prizes for gamers. Our project WeGame is entirely created for that.

First: The only thing users need to do is registering to our website for all of the features that we mentioned above. We targeted 1 mln+ user during the first 4 months and most of them will advise this app to their friends. As we calculated during the next 5 months (months 4-9) we can get more than 3.5 mln users.

Second: We have a plan for getting much more people using this app. People who use this app will be able to get prizes such as gift cards, equipments(for developers) etc. by using and sharing our app. All the people who share that will join a giveaway. With that, more people will hear about it and it will become more popular.
